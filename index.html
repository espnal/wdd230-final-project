Santo Domingo Temple

The Santo Domingo Temple, Dominican Republic, is one of the temples built and operated by the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, the 99th built by the church and the only one built in the Caribbean Islands, located in Los Robles, south of Santo Domingo, the capital of the Dominican Republic.

It has a total of 6,225 square meters of construction, with four rooms for LDS ordinances and four rooms for marriage sealings.

The Santo Domingo temple is used by members in congregations affiliated with the church in the Dominican Republic, Puerto Rico and Haiti.
